Credit Analysts I (2 positions open) – Analyze financial information to evaluate the risk for extending credit and loans to customers, including asset valuations, property appraisal reports, financial documents, and credit history. Review financial data and provide advisory reports regarding charged off accounts and personal guarantees, and investors/guarantors (Fannie Mae/SBA). Review credit applications for lending facilities to regulated fund entities and investment advisors. Generate financial ratios using analysis software to evaluate customers’ financial status. Assist in the development of workout and action plans through assessment of third-party vendors used by Borrowers. Research, develop and implement statistical models to predict and monitor credit risk trends. Conduct credit origination processes, including reviewing completed loan files for discrepancies in income, appraised value, and credit history. Monitor and analyze consumer credit quality and evaluate data for trends and potential opportunities. Evaluate the effectiveness of current loan and financial strategies and policies and provide recommendations and reports for improvement.

Position requires Master’s or foreign equivalent degree in Accounting, or Finance and a minimum one year of experience in above duties, or one year of broker assistance experience in the mortgage loan industry in the areas of real estate market research, analysis of impact and feasibility of various financing structures, conventional property evaluation, creditor conditions compliance, and portfolio management.
